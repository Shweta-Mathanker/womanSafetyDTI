Your Safety Matters

Empowering women with knowledge, resources, and tools for personal safety. Stay informed, stay prepared, stay safe.

Essential Safety Tips

Stay Connected

  • • Keep your phone charged and accessible
  • • Share your location with trusted contacts
  • • Install safety apps and emergency numbers

Public Safety

  • • Stay aware of your surroundings
  • • Walk confidently and stay in well-lit areas
  • • Trust your instincts

Workplace Safety

  • • Know your rights and company policies
  • • Report harassment immediately
  • • Document any concerning incidents
